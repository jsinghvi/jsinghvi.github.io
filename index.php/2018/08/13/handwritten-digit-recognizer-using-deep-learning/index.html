What is Deep Learning?

Deep Learning is essentially a subset of Machine Learning that aims to simulate the working of a human brain, i.e. designing of neural networks that use the power of modern day computers to perform actions comparable to or better than humans.

These actions may range from everyday tasks like distinguishing between a person or a dog, to predicting exoplanets across a galaxy.
