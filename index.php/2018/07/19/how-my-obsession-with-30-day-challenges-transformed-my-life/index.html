How My Obsession with 30 Day Challenges Transformed My Life

Posted July 19, 2018July 19, 2018 jsinghvi@blogger

See that kid? That’s who I have always been. The scared little guy who always overthinks about everything thrown at him, wondering if life is ever gonna get better.

Last November, I came across an interesting answer on Quora by John Michael Domingo where he mentioned the 30 Day Challenge routine. I was immediately intrigued.

Next thing I knew, I had embarked on a journey I had no idea would be so rewarding.

#1 — Cold Shower

The first challenge was taking cold showers. I knew it was going to be tough given the Winter season, but boy, it was terrifying!

The first few days showering was the most dreaded part of my routine. But what started off as a one time challenge still continues to be the first thing that gets my day going.

#2 — Learning Spanish

After completing (28/30) the cold shower challenge, I tried out my luck with Spanish. I had always wanted to learn Spanish but never really got to studying it with dedication or discipline.

So I scavenged the Internet for some audio lessons, joined a language exchange group and spent 1 hour daily to explore the intricacies of the most romantic language.

By the end of the month, I could comfortably talk with my new Spanish native friends about everyday stuff.

#3 — Reading Before Bed

Next up my to-do list was to dust off the books from my shelf and finally get around to reading them.

I decided to read everyday for about 30 minutes until I dozed off. This way I could avoid clinging on to my smartphone which usually turned my lets check out my facebook feed to me exploring theories on possible life on exoplanets.

# Takeaway

Sometimes it is better to start off, then plan.

You get the point, don’t you?

Thanks for reading this.
